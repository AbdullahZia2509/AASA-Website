Public Venues

Deliver interactive fan experiences and increase revenue with a robust, cutting-edge wireless network.

Tens of thousands of fans bring their mobile devices to games, concerts and events. All too often they have to deal with slow networks, if they are able to connect at all. The reason? Cell towers that typically cover this geographical area have reached a point of overcapacity. Once this happens to a cell tower, the local network becomes lagged and even inoperable.
A robust wireless infrastructure is needed to offload data traffic to more scalable wireless solutions, while also leading to new features and capabilities for the venue. Airtower Networks can provide your sports and entertainment venue with a network boost, additional capacity, and provide monetizing opportunities to expose untapped revenue through our robust wireless solution.
